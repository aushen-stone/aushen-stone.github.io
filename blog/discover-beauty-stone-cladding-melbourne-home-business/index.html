Are you looking to give your home or business a unique transformation? Look no further than Aushen Stone & Tile for all your requirements! Aesthetic appeal is vital in modern architecture, and stone cladding provides an artistic and beautiful solution suitable for any interior and exterior design project in Melbourne. At Aushen Stone & Tile, we use top-quality materials and unbeatable installation services that offer durability, waterproofing, and insulation to our residential and commercial clients.Builders, architects, and interior designers recognise us as industry leaders in wall-cladding services. Our innovative system provides a timeless look that is versatile and cost-effective and lets you experience functionality with style! From classic marble to Nevada, king stone, whistler, Uluru, Thredbo, and bluestone - we have an extensive selection of natural stone slabs for you.

The Importance of Aushen Stone and Tile Wall Cladding

Durable and Long-lasting:

Stone wall cladding provides a long-lasting solution that can withstand harsh weather and external damage, ensuring the stability of a building's architectural core.

Aesthetic Appeal:

Stone cladding brings an unparalleled artistic touch to any space, boasting stunning combinations of natural stones and textures. These statement walls are essential to exceptional interior design, making them an absolute must-have.

Easy Installation:

Prefabricated panels of stone wall cladding can be easily affixed to the top of the existing wall or structure without causing any impact on the building's stability.

Water Penetration Prevention:

Natural stone wall cladding is highly beneficial in preventing water penetration and drainage issues, safeguarding the structure from potential water damage.

Why Choose Us?

Expert assistance:

We won't let you miss this great opportunity! We have an expert team of professionals who guide you throughout the process, from selecting to installing the perfect stone cladding.

Client-centric approach:

We assist with designing, selecting, and installing the cladding to ensure our clients' satisfaction.

Cost-effective:

We value our customers' investment and deliver reliable, specific designs that meet our client's requirements.

Condensation Prevention:

Our beautiful range of natural stone wall cladding helps prevent condensation, ensuring a healthier indoor environment by managing moisture levels.
