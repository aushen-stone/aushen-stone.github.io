Today there are various ways in which artificial grass can be installed. Some of these ways are as under:</p><h3 id="installing-over-the-soil">Installing over the Soil</h3><p>Today soil is one of the most common options for building an artificial lawn. Garden area, play area, ground and other such places are where soil is found in abundance. Once you raise the level of the soil, you can install a synthetic turf over it. An installation of synthetic grass at such places gives it a warm and friendly vibe.</p><h3 id="installing-over-cement-pavements">Installing over Cement Pavements</h3><p>If you are planning to give your swimming pool and patio an artistic look then installing a synthetic turf is the best option. Opting for an artificial turf will give these places a native look.</p><h3 id="installing-over-wooden-surfaces">Installing over Wooden Surfaces</h3><p>Wooden surfaces are ideal for installing artificial grass.
