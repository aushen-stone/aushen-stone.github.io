Top 3 Outdoor Places To Use Porcelain Tiles

Porcelain tile is a popular flooring and wall covering material for many homes, offices and businesses worldwide. The perfect alternative to natural stone tiles and slabs...

Porcelain tile is a popular flooring and wall covering material for many homes, offices and businesses worldwide. The perfect alternative to natural stone tiles and slabs, porcelain is not only durable and easy to maintain but also comes in various styles and colours to suit your needs. Whether it is the balcony, entrance flooring or pool decks, porcelain tile in Melbourne will add visual appeal to any area of your premises.

Let's look at the top three places to use porcelain tiles to enhance your home aesthetics.

1. Balcony

Whether for sipping a cup of tea or relaxing, the balcony is one of the favourite places for people of all ages. It is, therefore, essential to have durable and weather-resistant tiles that can withstand rain, snow, and extreme temperatures without getting damaged or discoloured. Porcelain tiles are an excellent choice for the balcony because they are resistant to:

  1. Stains
  2. Heat
  3. Scratches
  4. Moisture
  5. Slip

One of the best things about porcelain tiles is that, unlike other stones, they come in a wide variety of colours, patterns and sizes, so you can choose the perfect style to match your balcony's decor.

2. Entrance

The entrance is one of the most important areas of a building because it sets the tone for the rest of the premises. It is also the first area that shows the visitors the personality and vibe of the homeowner, so it must be impressive. Whether you want a sleek, modern look or something more traditional, porcelain tiles can help create an attractive entranceway that suits your taste and style.

Porcelain tiles are highly durable that can withstand heavy foot traffic and extreme weather conditions. Additionally, their non-slip surface makes them a safe choice for entrance.

3. Pool Deck

Porcelain is also an excellent choice for enhancing the look of your outdoor areas, such as pool areas. Porcelain tile in Melbourne is designed to combat harsh Australian weather conditions without fading or cracking. They are resistant to water and chlorine, making them a safe choice for areas that may become wet or slippery.

A pool deck made with porcelain tiles can create a luxurious and sophisticated look. You can choose from various colours and styles to create an outdoor area that complements your pool's overall aesthetic.
